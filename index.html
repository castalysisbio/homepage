About Us

关于我们

Shanghai Castalysis Bioscience Co., Ltd. is an innovative biotechnology company focused on developing next-generation gene editing therapies.

上海篆码生物科技有限公司是一家专注于下一代基因编辑疗法开发的创新型生物科技公司。

Our Mission

我们的使命

We are committed to leveraging globally leading proprietary technology platforms to develop transformative treatments that offer "one-time treatment, lifelong cure" for patients worldwide. Our vision is to become a global leader in gene-coding solutions.

我们致力于利用全球领先的自主知识产权技术平台，开发"一次治疗，终生治愈"的变革性疗法，为全球患者提供创新治疗方案。我们的愿景是成为全球领先的基因编码解决方案提供商。

Core Technology

核心技术

Our proprietary alphaCas® (Cas12n) gene editing system represents a breakthrough in multiple dimensions compared to current mainstream technologies, with clear underlying patents, ultra-compact size, high efficiency and safety, extremely low immunogenicity, and unique targeting selectivity.

我们拥有完全自主开发的alphaCas® (Cas12n)基因编辑系统，与目前主流技术相比，在多个维度实现了突破性优势，具有底层专利清晰、超小型尺寸、高效且安全、极低的免疫原性和独特的靶向选择性等特点。

Laboratory Environment

Technology Platform

技术平台

Our proprietary alphaCas® gene editing platform offers multiple breakthrough advantages

我们拥有自主知识产权的alphaCas®基因编辑平台具有多重突破性优势

Clear Patent Position

底层专利清晰

alphaCas® is a novel gene editor originally discovered by our scientific founders, completely avoiding patent restrictions of existing technologies like Cas9.

alphaCas®是科学创始人团队原创发现的新型基因编辑器，完全规避了现有Cas9等技术的专利限制。

Ultra-Compact Size

超小型尺寸

The alphaCas® protein is less than 500 amino acids, only one-third the size of traditional Cas9, enabling efficient delivery with lower drug doses.

alphaCas®蛋白全长小于500个氨基酸，尺寸仅为传统Cas9的三分之一，能够以更低的药物剂量实现高效递送。

High Efficiency & Safety

高效且安全

Achieves >95% editing efficiency in multiple disease-related targets with significantly improved safety profile compared to traditional Cas9 editors.

在多个疾病相关靶点上实现>95%的编辑效率，经金标准验证显著优于传统Cas9编辑器，具有卓越的安全性。

Low Immunogenicity

极低的免疫原性

Derived from rare deep-sea microorganisms with extremely low pre-existing antibody levels in the human population.

来源于罕见深海微生物，在人群中预存抗体比例极低，免疫原性远低于常见病原菌来源的编辑器。

Unique Targeting Selectivity

独特的靶向选择性

Recognizes unique AAN-type PAM sequences, providing significant differentiation from Cas9 (NGG) and Cas12a (TTTV).

识别独特的AAN型PAM序列，与Cas9(NGG)、Cas12a(TTTV)等形成显著差异，提供巨大的靶点差异化空间。

One-Time Treatment

一次性治疗

Our gene editing medicines aim to achieve "one-shot cure" compared to traditional preventive therapies that require frequent dosing.

相比传统需要频繁给药的预防性疗法，我们的基因编辑药物旨在实现"一针治愈"。
